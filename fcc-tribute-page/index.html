Here's the line of Hon. Daniel Toroitich Arap Moi's Life:

  • 1924 - Born in Sacho,Baringo,kenya Colony.
  • 1934 - Moi attended his schooling at tandui primary school which was then a mission school.Helater moved to the African Inland Mission School Kabartojo in the same year.
  • 1942 - Joined Kapsabet Government African School ,present day Kapsabet Boys
  • 1945 - Finished his secondary school education and later joined a teacher's training school in Kapsabet where he also double as student teacher.
  • 1955 - Moi made the first step to politics when he was among the eight to be selected to fill the position of Joseph Oletemano who had resign from the position of Rift valley constituency ,a position he won.
  • 1957 - Formed Kenys National Union of Teachers ,because techers were subjected to harsh working conditions,
  • 1960 - Moi joined other leaders to participate in constitutional task that were held in London. Later formed Kenya National African Union (KANU).
  • 1964 - Elected to be the Member Of Parliament and the Minister of Home Affairs.
  • 1967 - Moi became the 3rdVice President of Kenys.
  • 1978 - Moi bacame the 2ndPresident of Kenya ,where he ruled for 24 good years.
  • 1981 - Elected to be the Chairman Of OAU Preceded by Saika Stevens.
  • 2020 - Dies at the age of 96.
